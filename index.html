Atlantic Ocean

Special: The second-largest ocean, it separates the Americas from Europe and Africa. It is known for its significant shipping routes, the Bermuda Triangle, and the Gulf Stream.

Indian Ocean

Special: The third-largest ocean, it is bordered by Asia, Africa, Australia, and the Indian subcontinent. It is important for trade routes and is known for its warm waters and monsoon winds.

Arctic Ocean

Special: The smallest and shallowest of the world's oceans, it is located around the Arctic region and is mostly covered by sea ice. It is critical for global climate and has unique marine life.

Southern Ocean

Special: Also known as the Antarctic Ocean, it surrounds Antarctica and is recognized for its strong currents, such as the Antarctic Circumpolar Current, and for being the least explored ocean.

Pacific Ocean
